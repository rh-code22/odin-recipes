Tuna Casserole

Description

Oh man, rainy day comfort food in a 9 x 13" dish. "Cheese and fish?", you might exclaim, "well that sounds awful!" But I dissent!

Noodles, vegetables, and canned tuna fish are baked together in a creamy sauce and topped with crispy potato chips.

Makes 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked noodles (wide egg noodles are fine, or macaroni)
  • 1 TB butter
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas, defrosted
  • 1 can tuna (5-6 oz, drained)
  • 10.5 oz can of cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 or 3 cups of potato chips, crushed
  • 1 TB butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F
  2. Cook noodles al dente according to package directions. Drain & rinse.
  3. In empty noodle pot, cook onion and celery in butter until tender, about 5 mins.
  4. Once tender, turn off heat and add the drained noodles, peas, soup, milk, cheese, and tuna to pot. Mix well.
  5. Dump everything into a 9 x 13" baking dish (2qts) and top with crumbled potato chips, parm, and melted butter.
  6. Bake 18-20 minutes until bubbly and the potato chips are starting to brown.
  7. Let cool 10 minutes before serving.
