Banana Bread

Description

A not-too-sweet quickbread that makes a delicious breakfast treat or snack.

This is a great way to use up over-ripe bananas.

Makes about 10 slices

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 cups mashed ripe banana (about 2 or 3 bananas)
  • 1/3 c butter, melted (76 grams)
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3/4 cup white sugar. You can use 1/2 cup instead if you prefer less sweet (100 - 150 grams)
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 c All-purpose flour (205 grams)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and butter an 8 x 4" loaf pan.
  2. Mix together banana and butter.
  3. Sprinkle baking soda and salt over banana mixture & mix.
  4. Add egg, sugar, and vanilla & mix.
  5. Finally, add flour and stir just until the flour is not visible - try not to overmix.
  6. Bake 50 minutes to 1 hour, or until a tester inserted in the middle of the cake comes out clean.
